# Monetization

How Wavvon sustains itself while staying free for everyone, forever.

**Current answer**: donations. No centralized revenue mechanism is built
into the software or protocol. The approach to long-term sustainability is
an open question — other paths (grants, commercial support, community
funding) can be explored without adding any code to the protocol.

---

## Constraints (permanent)

- No subscriptions. No premium tiers. No capability ever locked behind
  money.
- A user with a zero balance must have an identical product experience to
  anyone else.
- Community hubs the project does not operate must never be touched by
  money flows. A hub admin should never have to think about billing.
- Forks and self-hosters are first-class. No monetization surface may be
  mandatory or protocol-level.

---

## Funding mechanism — Donations

Simple, voluntary, no perks. A donate link on the project website.
Donations are the most ethos-aligned mechanism: no sponsor relationship,
no hosting lock-in, no in-software changes required.

There are no donation-gated cosmetics or "supporter flair" — tying any
in-software item to money reintroduces complexity and ethos drift. The
donate link goes to the project's open-source funding page; that is the
entire surface.

---

## Mechanisms explicitly rejected

- **Subscriptions / premium tiers** — splits users into paying and
  non-paying, which works only by making the free product deliberately
  worse. See [`decisions.md`](decisions.md).
- **In-chat or in-voice advertising** — rejected on no-surveillance and
  channel-as-place grounds. The conversation surface stays ad-free.
- **Selling telemetry or user data** — rejected on sovereignty grounds.
- **Missions / sponsor-funded sparks** — removed. Required a
  Wavvon-operated central service and introduced a permanent sponsor
  relationship into the software. See [`decisions.md`](decisions.md).
- **Project-operated farm hosting as a revenue line** — the project
  publishes farm software; anyone can run a farm. A Wavvon-operated
  commercial farm is an independent business decision, not a design
  constraint. See [`decisions.md`](decisions.md).
- **Paid-placement in hub or gaming discovery** — distorts organic
  recommendations. If ever revisited, must be visibly labelled and kept
  separate from organic results.
